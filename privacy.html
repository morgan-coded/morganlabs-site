Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026

MorganLabs ("MorganLabs," "we," "our," or "us") operates the StockLock Shopify app ("StockLock" or the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when merchants install and use StockLock.

1. Information we collect

When you install or use StockLock, we may collect and store the following information from your Shopify store:

  • Store information, such as your shop domain
  • Product and inventory information needed for inventory synchronization
  • SKU and inventory item identifiers
  • App configuration and mapping data created by the merchant
  • Technical and diagnostic information, such as logs related to app performance, sync events, and errors

We do not intentionally collect or process customer personal information unless it is incidentally included in data provided by Shopify or necessary for app operation.

2. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide inventory synchronization across connected Shopify stores
  • Maintain SKU mappings and related configuration
  • Process inventory updates and sync events
  • Troubleshoot issues, improve reliability, and monitor app performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How we share information

We do not sell merchant data.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers that help us host, operate, monitor, or support the App
  • When required by law, regulation, subpoena, or legal process
  • To protect the security, integrity, or rights of MorganLabs, merchants, Shopify, or others

4. Data retention

We retain data only as long as needed to operate the App, provide support, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, and enforce our agreements. We may delete or anonymize data when it is no longer needed.

5. Data security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Merchant responsibilities

Merchants are responsible for ensuring they have the right to use Shopify and any store data connected to the App. Merchants are also responsible for configuring the App appropriately for their business operations.

7. Third-party services

StockLock works with Shopify and may rely on third-party infrastructure or hosting providers to operate. Your use of Shopify is also subject to Shopify's own terms and privacy practices.

8. Your choices

If you uninstall the App, we may continue to retain certain data for a limited period as necessary for legal compliance, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, or legitimate business operations. You may contact us to request deletion of retained data, subject to applicable legal requirements.

9. Children's privacy

The App is intended for business use by merchants and is not directed to children.
